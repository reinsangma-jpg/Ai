About MoneyFlow:

MoneyFlow Ultimate is a comprehensive personal finance management system designed to help individuals track their finances, investments, and achieve their financial goals. Built with modern web technologies and a focus on user experience.

Features:

  • Complete account management (Cash, Bank, Demat)
  • Stock portfolio tracking with realistic ₹20 brokerage
  • Asset management (FD, Insurance, Property, Vehicles)
  • F&O trading records and P/L tracking
  • Budget planning and goal setting
  • Detailed analytics with interactive charts
  • Dark mode support with persistence
  • Complete data export and backup system
  • Keyboard shortcuts for power users
  • Achievement system for milestones

Version: 5.0 (2026)
